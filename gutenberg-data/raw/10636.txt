This is clearly "The Travels of Marco Polo" in the famous Yule-Cordier edition. Henry Yule originally translated and annotated Marco Polo's travels, and Henri Cordier later revised and expanded this work. The text shows this is the third edition from 1903, with Cordier's additional notes from 1920.

Marco Polo's original travels took place between approximately 1271-1295, and he dictated his account to Rustichello da Pisa while imprisoned in Genoa in 1298-1299. The work was originally titled "Livre des merveilles du monde" (Book of the Marvels of the World) or "Il Milione."
