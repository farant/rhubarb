The original **Bagh O Bahar** was written by **Mir Amman of Delhi** (Mir Amman Dihlawi) in 1801-1802 at the Fort William College in Calcutta. It was commissioned as a textbook for teaching Hindustani to British officials. Mir Amman adapted it from earlier Persian tales, particularly the "Qissa-e Chahar Darvesh" (Tale of Four Dervishes).
