Published in 1921, this compilation serves as a poignant memorial to the French soldiers who made the ultimate sacrifice. The letters reveal the idealistic and pure spirit in which these soldiers faced death, demonstrating their conscious and willing sacrifice for their nation. It stands as a monument to the imperishable glory of the French soldier.
